Video lectures from Fall 2016
Lecture Notes from 2016.
  • Part I : Management and course policies. Lecture 1.
  • Part II : Notation, important matrices, errors, stability, conditioning. Lectures 2 - 4
  • Part III : Introduction to linear equations, Banach Lemma.
  • Part IVa: Gaussian Elimination, general matrices, pivoting, errors.
  • Part IVb: Gaussian Elimination, problems with special structure.
  • Part Va: Linear Least Squares, normal equations, QR decomposition.
  • Part Vb: Linear Least Squares, Singular Value Decomposition.
  • Part VIa: Stationary Iterative Methods for Linear Equations.
  • Part VIb: Krylov methods intro; GMRES theory and implementation.
  • Part VIc: GMRES Examples.
  • Part VId: Conjugate Gradient Iteration. Lectues
  • Part VIIa: Nonlinear Equations, Picard iteration, Newton's method.
  • Part VIIb: Newton-Krylov Methods, Global Convergence.
  • Part VIIc: Nonlinear Least Squares.
  • Part VIIIa: Eigenvalue Problems, Power Method, Rayleigh Quotient iteration.
  • Part VIIIb: Eigenvalue conditioning.
  • Part IXa: Residual Correction.
  • Part IXb: Multigrid.
