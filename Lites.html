LITES Home Page

Documentation and code for LITES

If you are reading this you should be part of one of the research groups who use and develop LITES. This means you are or collaborate with at least one of Alexei, Dave, Dwight, James, Jerry, Tim, or Weidong. This software is still under development and we are not prepared to support it outside the group.

Please do not share the link for this page with others.

Getting started:
  • Contact Tim to
    • Get an account on the NCSU cluster and get permission to use our high-priority queues.
    • Get onto the Gaussian license.
  • Move the tar file onto your cluster account and unpack it.
  • READ THE DOCUMENTATION and get the butene example to run.
  • Contact Weidong if you have problems with the example. This example helps you get your files organized.
