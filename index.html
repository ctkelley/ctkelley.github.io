My NC State career (1978-2025) is fading away.
I will leave my office at 3234 SAS Hall no later than June 30, 2025.

NC State, it's been a pleasure doing business with you. Thank you very much.

My post-retirement research interests are in non-smooth optimization, linear/nonlinear equations, mixed-precision algorithms, and particle transport problems. I continue to publish in these areas.

In the past I worked on multilevel methods for integral equations, quasi-Newton methods, semiconductor modeling, optimal control, optimization of noisy functions, flow in porous media, and computational quantum chemistry and physics.

I distribute MATLAB software for Linear Equations, Nonlinear Equations, and Optimization . I have new Julia software for Nonlinear equations.
