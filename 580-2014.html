This is the home page for MA 580. Most of the data here will also be on the course Moodle page. If you are not an NCSU student, then Moodle means nothing to you. Enjoy the lectures and the video recordings.

Lecture Notes from 2014.
  • Part I : Management and course policies. Lecture 1.
  • Part II : Notation, important matrices, errors, stability, conditioning. Lectures 2 - 4
  • Part III : Introduction to linear equations, Banach Lemma . Lecture 5.
  • Part IVa: Gaussian Elimination, general matrices, pivoting, errors. Lectures 6 - 8
  • Part IVb Gaussian Elimination, problems with special structure. Lectures 8 - 10
  • Part Va: Linear Least Squares Problems, Normal Equations, QR factorization. Lectures 9 - 12
  • Part Vb: Linear Least Squares Problems, Singular Value Decomposition. Lectures 12 - 14
  • Part VIa: Stationary Iterative Methods for Linear Equations, Boltzmann Transport Equation. Lectures 15-17.
  • Part VIb: Krylov Methods Introduction, GMRES. Lectures 17-18.
  • Part VIc: GMRES Examples, Lectures 19-21.
  • Part VId: Conjugate Gradient and low-storage Krylov iterations, Lectures 21-22.
  • Part VIIa: Nonlinear Equations and Newton's Method. Lectures 22-24.
  • Part VIIb: Newton-Krylov Methods and Global Convergence. Lectures 25-26
  • Part VIIc: Nonlinear least squares. Lecture 26
  • Part VIIIa: Eigenvalue Problems, Power Method, Rayleigh Quotient Iteration. Lectures 27-28.
  • Part VIIIb: Eigenvalue Conditioning, QR Algorithm. Lectures 29-30.
  • Part IXa: Residual Correction Methods and Multigrid. Lectues 30 - 31.
  • Part IXb: Multigrid. Lectures 31-34.
  • Part IXc: FFT and applications. Lectures 34-37.
  • Part IXd: Continuation. Lectures 37-39.
