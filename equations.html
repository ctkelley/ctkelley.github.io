Linear and Nonlinear Equations

Linear/Nonlinear Equations and Multilevel Methods

This is a summary of our activity in equation solving and least squares problems. This work involves multilevel methods, Krylov methods, and global convergence. Some of this activity is related to my work on porous media flow .
