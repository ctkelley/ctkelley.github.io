Porous Media Flow

Tim Kelley's Project on Porous Media Flow

In this project we work on algorithmic issues (linear and nonlinear equations, temporal integration, preconditioner design, parallelism) in simulation of subsurface flow and transport and on optimization problems that arise in design of algorithms. This project is or has been supported by
  • the Army Research Office ,
  • the US Army Engineer Research and Development Center,
  • the National Science Foundation, (Check out our ITR project ).
  • the US Dept of Education GAANN fellowship program, and
  • the Cray Fellowship program
